About

Parity Technologies, Web3 Foundation, and Wanxiang Blockchain Labs are joining to create a six-month bootcamp to support Substrate-based projects.

Select bootcamp projects will be considered for parachain and parathreads in Wanxiang's POC program.

Wanxiang brings partners and their application use case scenarios, which include smart city, automobile and supply chain finance, and public services.

Who should apply?

  • Teams building on Substrate who want to access the Chinese market and top-level corporates

Program overview

  1. Stage 1: Meet and Discover (1 week)

    Meet key corporate personnel and the committee council. Initial discussion of direction to prepare for the POC application on opening ceremony week. Introduces core project technology.

  2. Stage 2: Acceleration and Development (2.5 months)

    A rich acceleration program to enhance team's expertise in Substrate architecture, product design, top tier investor and entrepreneurs share fundraising experience and guidance, legal compliance, access local talent, and engage with Chinese and international community.

  3. Stage 3: Delivery and Feedback (2 weeks)

    Prepare a pilot delivery for demo day, pitch their proposal to the relevant stakeholders and decision makers as a candidate for a parachain. Qualified teams prepare to move into POC.

  4. Stage 4: POC Brainstorm and Design (2-3 weeks)

    Projects and corporate partners explore specific ideas for pilot POC proposals. Corporate partners ensure start-up team have access to relevant industry expertise and data in order to fully develop their proposals.

  5. Stage 5: Agreement Draft and Development

    Projects and corporates work together to draft an agreement (defining required resources, timelines and key deliverables)

  6. Stage 6: Graduation
