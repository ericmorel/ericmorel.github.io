Your blockchain for the future.

Cross-language support with WebAssembly

WebAssembly is the next web standard. Because Substrate’s runtime is compiled to WebAssembly, you can write your blockchain logic in any language that can compile to it (Rust, Go, C/C++, C#, etc).

Mobile first, light-client friendly

Traditional blockchains have retrofitted light clients onto their networks, but Substrate comes with a light client built in from day one. Use your mobile-ready blockchain anywhere, whether it’s for identity, IoT, or your data.

Forkless upgrades

Adding features to decentralized blockchains has never been easier. With Substrate, everybody stays on the same path without the risk of forking. Because blocks can be processed on-chain with WebAssembly, out-of-date clients are always able to stay on the same chain without forking.

Built-in coordination

Coordinating all the participants of a network upgrade can be a daunting task. Substrate comes with the tools to help networks decide which upgrades to implement. Whether you want to make decisions with the help of an elected council, public referendum, or sophisticated governance mechanism, Substrate gives you the most comprehensive suite of tools for coordination.

Instant finality

Blockchains provide guarantees that data will not be lost or modified ad-hoc, and instant finality ensures your network reaches consensus in seconds, not minutes or hours. Substrate’s GRANDPA algorithm reaches and finalizes consensus quickly, across networks big and small.

Seamless integration

Don’t shape your solution to fit a blockchain, shape your blockchain to fit your solution. Off-chain workers help integrate your existing data, logic, and other complex tasks into your blockchain with low overhead. Learn more here.

A network of blockchains

Access the Polkadot network

Substrate blockchains are natively compatible with Polkadot, making it simple to become a parachain or parathread and tap into Polkadot’s shared security. Cumulus, Substrate’s tool for connecting your blockchain to a network of blockchains, unlocks interchain communication, collaboration, and shared security.
